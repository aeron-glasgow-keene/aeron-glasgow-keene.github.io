Te Whare Tapa Wha - My Health Plan while at Dev Academy [BOOTCAMP UPDATE]

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

I will: go to the gym 3 times a week (unless unwell or injured); do yoga at least twice a week; eat healthy and go to bed before 11pm every weeknight.

Taha Whānau (Family/Social Wellbeing)

I will keep in touch with friends and family, and go on at least one social outing per week when possible.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

I will meditate for at least 5 mins a day at least 5 times a week. I will continue journalling every night and attending my Men's Group every fortnight. I will go to campus for the day at least 90% of the time during the week. I will adhere to the same weekly schedule as much as possible.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

I will do at least 1 hour of music production and at least 10 minutes of gratitude journalling per week.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

I will stay mindful of the natural world during my walk to and from campus every day. I will go outside on my breaks when there is sunshine (on those rare Wellington winter days when there is any) for at least 20 mins.

Te Whare Tapa Wha - My Health Plan while at Dev Academy [FOUNDATIONS]

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

I will go on a walk first thing every morning and do at least 10 mins of yoga every day.

Taha Whānau (Family/Social Wellbeing)

I will keep in touch with friends and family, and go on at least one social outing per week when possible.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

I will meditate for at least 5 mins a day (before coffee! No coffee without meditating!). Journalling every night and attending my Men's Group every fortnight.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

I will do at least 2 hours of music production and at least 10 minutes of gratitude journalling per week.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Other than my daily morning walk, I will prioritise being out in the sunshine (on those rare Wellington winter days when there is any) for at least an hour - either walking somehwere or working on course stuff on the grass/outdoor furniture in the yard.
